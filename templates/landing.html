How AttendGuard Works

A simple, three-step process that saves hours every week

1

Set Up Your School

Add students, instructors, and class schedules. Our admin panel makes bulk imports and role management a breeze. Invite your team and they're ready to go.

2

Record Attendance

Instructors open the portal, select their class, and mark students present, absent, or late with one tap. The system logs everything automatically with timestamps and notes.

3

Notify & Analyze

Parents receive instant alerts. Admins view real-time dashboards and attendance reports. Export data for accreditation or insights—all in one click.
